The Creator

Robert Porter

I began this world as a way to combine all of the adventures in "The Tails of the Yawning Portal" campaign module. As the number of campaigns taking place within this world grew, I decided to flesh out the setting some more and it sort of snowballed from there. Now there are several thousand years of history, NPCs that many of the players have come to know and love (or hate).

The world grew to the point that I decided to take it to a more professional level. Since that time I have added four more groups to the world and Dungeon Master five nights a week.
