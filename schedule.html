Fees & Rules

All game times are $30/h(per party). There is a $5/h cancellation fee. All characters are to be made with Official D&D 5e content only. First session will be a 1 hour consultation/ "Session 0" to establish expectations of both the player and the DM. "Session 0" fee is $40.

All campaigns have an affect on each other. Affects will be applied during the Prep Q&A Recap hour. Players will be asked to join as a guest on the Grathia Podcast. Each time a player joins as a guest they earn their party two free hours of game play.

Above all else: Be Respectful to one another. Bigotry earns an Instantaneous and Eternal Ban!
