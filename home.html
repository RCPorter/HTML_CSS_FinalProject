The Grathia Campaign

What is Grathia?

Grathia is a Fantasy World created by Robert Porter in 2020. As a complete Campaign Setting, Grathia has detailed Regions, Cities, Dungeons, complete with a wide variety of Non-Player Character and a rich History!

When you play in the land of Grathia you find yourself in a mystical world populated by a wild array of biomes, beasts, and humanoid species. Each with their own desires, goals, and history.

What is Dungeons & Dragons?

The Best description of Dungeons & Dragons is a Collaborative Storytelling Experience. The game has been bringing friends together to create epic stories and unforgettable memories since Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson designed it in 1974.

Since its most recent release (5th Edition or "5e"), Dungeons and Dragons has exploded in popularity and has gone from a "satanic panic" to being showcased in television commercials.
